From Naples

Take US-41 (Tamiami Trail) east approximately 35 miles. We're on the south side of the road at 32016 Tamiami Trail East.

From Miami

Take US-41 (Tamiami Trail) west approximately 75 miles. We're on the south side of the road at 32016 Tamiami Trail East.

Parking & Arrival

Free parking available at meeting locations. Please arrive 15 minutes early for your scheduled tour. Your guide will brief you on safety and provide all equipment.

Nearby Lodging

The Dolphin View is a waterfront 5th wheel directly on the water, just steps from the tours. Also recommended: Outdoor Resorts, Ivey House, The Captain's Table, and River Wilderness.
